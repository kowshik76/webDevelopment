Akira Toriyama

Legendary Manga Artist and Creator of Dragon Ball

Biography

Akira Toriyama is a renowned Japanese manga artist and character designer best known for creating the iconic "Dragon Ball" series. He has also worked on "Dr. Slump" and contributed to several video games, including the famous RPG "Chrono Trigger". His innovative art style and engaging storytelling have made him one of the most influential figures in anime and manga history.

Notable Works

  • Dragon Ball (1984–1995)
  • Dragon Ball Z (1989–1996)
  • Dr. Slump (1980–1984)
  • Chrono Trigger (1995)
  • Dragon Quest (character designs)
