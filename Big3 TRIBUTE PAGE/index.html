The Big 3 Anime Writers

A Tribute to Eiichiro Oda, Masashi Kishimoto, and Tite Kubo

Eiichiro Oda

Eiichiro Oda is the creator of *One Piece*, one of the best-selling manga series of all time. His pirate-themed adventure has captivated fans worldwide since its debut in 1997.

Masashi Kishimoto

Masashi Kishimoto is best known for *Naruto*, which follows the story of a young ninja seeking recognition. Naruto's story of perseverance and friendship became a global sensation.

Tite Kubo

Tite Kubo is the mastermind behind *Bleach*, a series filled with soul reapers, hollows, and thrilling battles. Kubo's unique art style and storytelling have left a lasting impact on the anime world.
